## Role Overview

The **Student Portal** is a self-service platform that gives enrolled students direct access to all their official school records, real-time account balance, and learning resources — from any device, 24/7.

**What students can do:**
- View their grades and download their report card
- Check their school fee balance and full payment history
- Pay school fees online (GCash, Maya, credit/debit card, bank transfer)
- Access class materials uploaded by their teachers
- Take flashcard quizzes assigned by teachers
- Track which enrollment requirements have been submitted
- Re-enroll online for the next school year
- View their clinic/health history

**What students cannot do:**
- Change their personal information (contact the Registrar)
- View other students' data
- Change their section or grade level

## Workflow 1 — Logging In for the First Time

**URL:** `/portal-login`

---

### Step 1.1 — Receive Your Login Credentials

Your username and initial password are provided by the school's Registrar when your enrollment is confirmed. You will typically receive this:
- Printed on your enrollment confirmation slip, OR
- Via the contact number or email provided during registration

If you did not receive credentials, approach the Registrar's office.

---

### Step 1.2 — First Login

1. Go to the student portal login page: `/portal-login`
2. Enter your **username** and the **initial password** given by the school.
3. Click **Login**.

---

### Step 1.3 — Change Your Password

On first login, you are strongly encouraged to change your password to something private.

**Navigate to:** My Profile → Change Password

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|---------------|
| Current Password | The initial password given by the school |
| New Password | Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters, mix of letters and numbers) |
| Confirm New Password | Retype the new password |

> **Keep your password private.** Do not share it with classmates. If you suspect your account has been accessed by someone else, contact the Registrar immediately.

## Workflow 3 — Viewing Grades and Report Card

**Navigate to:** Student → Grades

---

### Step 3.1 — View Grades by Quarter/Period

Your grades are organized by:
- **School Year** (e.g., 2026-2027)
- **Subject** (e.g., Mathematics, English, Science)
- **Grading Period** (1st Quarter, 2nd Quarter, 3rd Quarter, 4th Quarter)

Grades appear only after your teacher has **submitted** them. If a grading period shows blank, it means your teacher has not yet released the grades. Contact your teacher or the Registrar if you believe grades should already be visible.

---

### Step 3.2 — View Your Report Card

**Navigate to:** Student → Report Card

Your report card shows all subjects and grades for the current semester/ year in the official report format.

---

### Step 3.3 — Download the Report Card as PDF

Click **Download PDF**. Save the file to your device. This is your official report card — you can present it to scholarship offices, transfer applications, or other institutions that request it.

> **Note:** The downloadable report card carries your school's official header and the registered school year data. It is a legitimate document. If an institution requires a physically signed and stamped copy, request it from the Registrar.

## Workflow 4 — Checking Your Ledger and Balance

**Navigate to:** Student → Ledger

---

### Step 4.1 — Understand Your Ledger

Your ledger shows a complete transaction history of your school account:

| Column | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| Date | When the charge was assessed or payment was recorded |
| Description | What the charge or payment is for (e.g., "Tuition Fee — 1st Month", "Payment — OR#00123") |
| Charge | Amount billed |
| Payment | Amount paid |
| Balance | Running balance (positive = you owe; zero = fully paid) |

---

### Step 4.2 — Identify Outstanding Balances

Scroll to the bottom to see your **Current Balance**. Charges with no corresponding payment are your outstanding obligations.

---

### Step 4.3 — View Previous School Year Ledger

Use the school year filter at the top to view your ledger history from previous years. This helps when verifying that a carried-forward balance was correctly transferred.

## Workflow 5 — Making an Online Payment

**Navigate to:** Student → Ledger → Pay Online (or Student → Enrollment → Pay Now)

**Available payment channels:**
- **GCash** — Most common; requires active GCash account
- **Maya** — Maya digital wallet
- **Credit / Debit Card** — Visa, Mastercard
- **Bank Transfer** — BDO, BPI, UnionBank, and other major banks (manual validation required)

---

### Step 5.1 — Select Items to Pay

Choose which fee items you want to pay in this transaction. You can pay one item, multiple items, or your full balance in one session.

---

### Step 5.2 — Choose Your Payment Method

Select your preferred payment channel. The system generates a secure payment link (powered by PayMongo) or, for bank transfer, shows the school's bank account details.

---

### Step 5.3A — GCash / Maya / Card Payment

1. Click the payment method.
2. You are redirected to the secure payment page.
3. Log in to GCash/Maya or enter your card details.
4. Confirm the payment.
5. You are returned to the portal. Your balance updates **immediately** once payment is confirmed.

---

### Step 5.3B — Bank Transfer Payment

1. Select **Bank Transfer**.
2. The school's bank account details are displayed.
3. Transfer the exact amount using your bank's mobile app or online banking.
4. Return to the portal and click **I've completed the transfer**.
5. Upload a screenshot or photo of your transfer confirmation (showing reference number, amount, recipient, and date).
6. Submit for cashier validation.

**Important:** Bank transfers require manually validation by the school Cashier. Your balance will update once the Cashier approves the submission. This typically takes **1–3 hours during school hours**.

---

### Step 5.4 — Save Your Receipt

After a successful payment, your official receipt is available in:
- Student → Ledger → payment line → View Receipt
- The confirmation notification in your portal inbox

Download or screenshot the receipt and keep it for your records.

## Workflow 6 — Applying a Discount Code

**When to do this:** If the school has given you a scholarship code, sibling discount code, or a promotional enrollment code.

**Navigate to:** Student → Ledger → Apply Discount Code (or during Online Payment checkout)

---

### Step 6.1 — Enter the Code

Type the discount code exactly as provided (codes are case-sensitive). Click **Apply**.

The system validates the code and shows you:
- Which fee items will receive the discount
- The discount amount
- Your new balance after the discount

---

### Step 6.2 — Confirm

Click **Confirm** to apply the discount permanently to your account. The discount appears as a credit entry in your ledger.

> **One-time use:** Most discount codes can only be used once. After applying, the code becomes invalid. Do not share your code with other students — if it is used by someone else first, you will not be able to apply it.

## Workflow 7 — Checking and Submitting Enrollment Requirements

**Navigate to:** Student → Enrollment → Requirements

---

### Step 7.1 — View Requirements Checklist

This page shows every required document for your enrollment, with status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ Submitted | The Registrar has confirmed receipt of this document |
| ⏳ Pending | Not yet submitted or not yet verified |
| ⚠️ Waived | The school has formally waived this requirement for you (documented on file) |

---

### Step 7.2 — Submit a Document Online

For requirements that allow digital submission:
1. Click **Upload** next to the requirement.
2. Upload a clear scan or photo of the document (PDF or JPG, minimum 300dpi for legibility).
3. Submit.

The Registrar reviews the upload and marks it as submitted once verified.

**Note:** For requirements that must be physically submitted (original documents), you still need to bring the original to the Registrar's office. The online upload is a pre-screening step only.

## Workflow 8 — Online Re-Enrollment

**When to do this:** At the start of each new school year when the school opens re-enrollment.

**Navigate to:** Student → Enrollment → Re-Enroll

---

### Step 8.1 — Verify Your Information

The system shows your current personal details. Review that your:
- Name, date of birth, and address are correct
- Contact number and email are current
- Guardian information is up to date

If any information is wrong, note the corrections and report them to the Registrar (you cannot change these yourself — changes require official documentation).

---

### Step 8.2 — Select Your Grade Level (if applicable)

For continuing students, the system auto-fills your next grade level based on your completed grade. If you need a different strand/track (for Grade 10 → 11 transition), select it here.

---

### Step 8.3 — Submit the Re-Enrollment Request

Click **Submit Re-Enrollment**. Your request goes to the Registrar for confirmation and section assignment.

After the Registrar confirms:
- Your enrollment status changes to `For Assessment`
- The Accounting team assigns your fee schedule
- Your status updates to `For Payment`, then `Enrolled` after payment

## Workflow 9 — Viewing Class Materials and Announcements

**Navigate to:** Student → Materials (or Student → Announcements)

---

### Step 9.1 — Browse Materials by Class

Materials are organized by class and subject. Each file shows:
- Title
- Uploaded date
- File type (PDF, document, video link, etc.)

Click a material to open or download it. All materials are published by your subject teacher. If you expect a material and it is not showing, check with your teacher — it may still be in draft.

---

### Step 9.2 — Read Announcements

**Navigate to:** Student → Announcements

Announcements from all your enrolled classes appear in this feed, sorted by date (newest first). Pinned announcements appear at the top regardless of date.

Click an announcement to read the full text. Unread announcements are highlighted.

## Workflow 10 — Taking a Flashcard Quiz

**Navigate to:** Student → Flashcards

---

### Step 10.1 — View Available Decks

Decks assigned to your classes appear here with:
- Deck title and subject
- Mode (Practice or Quiz)
- Deadline (for quiz mode)
- Completion status (Not Started / In Progress / Completed)

---

### Step 10.2 — Practice Mode

Practice mode lets you study at your own pace with no scoring.

1. Click **Practice**.
2. Cards are shown one at a time. The front (question/term) is shown.
3. Click **Flip** or press spacebar to reveal the answer.
4. Mark each card as **Got It** or **Still Learning**.
5. Cards marked "Still Learning" cycle back for another review round.
6. Continue until you've reviewed all cards.

---

### Step 10.3 — Quiz Mode

Quiz mode is graded and timed.

1. Click **Take Quiz**.
2. Read the instructions: number of questions, time limit, maximum attempts.
3. Click **Start**.
4. Answer each question by typing or selecting the answer.
5. Click **Submit** when finished (or the quiz auto-submits when time runs out).
6. Your score is shown immediately.
7. Review which questions you answered correctly and which were wrong.

**Your score is recorded** and visible to your teacher. If the quiz allows multiple attempts, your best score is typically used.

## Workflow 11 — Downloading Official Documents

**Navigate to:** Student → Enrollment → Documents

| Document | What It Is | When You Need It |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Enrollment Certificate** | Proof that you are officially enrolled for the current school year | Scholarship applications, bank accounts for minors, transfer to another school inquiry |
| **Report Card (PDF)** | Official grade report for the current or previous year | Same as above; college applications |
| **Form 137 (SF10)** | Permanent record — cumulative academic history | College entrance, transfer, graduation requirements |

> **Form 137** can only be released officially by the Registrar with a signature and school seal. The download here is a system-generated copy for reference. For the official sealed copy, request it at the Registrar's office.

## Workflow 12 — Viewing Your Health Record

**Navigate to:** Student → Health Record (if enabled by the school nurse)

Your health record shows:
- Blood type, height, weight history
- Known allergies and medical conditions
- Vaccination records
- Clinic visit history (complaints, treatment, medicines given)

This record is maintained by the School Nurse. You **cannot edit** this data — contact the clinic if you find an error.

## Workflow 13 — Clearance Status

**Navigate to:** Student → Clearance

Clearance is required for specific transactions (e.g., releasing Form 137, graduation processing, transferring to another school).

Your clearance card shows each department/office with their clearance status:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| ✅ Cleared | The office has confirmed you have no outstanding obligations |
| ❌ Not Cleared | You have a pending obligation with this office |
| ⏳ Pending | Clearance not yet processed by this office |

**Common reasons for not-cleared status:**

| Office | Common Reason |
|--------|--------------|
| Accounting | Outstanding school fees balance |
| Library | Unreturned book or unpaid library fine |
| Clinic | Unreturned clinic materials |
| Registrar | Missing required documents |
| Custodian | School property not returned |

Resolve any outstanding obligation directly with the concerned office, then ask them to update your clearance in the system.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Cannot log in | Wrong username or password | Check caps lock; contact Registrar for password reset |
| Grade not showing | Teacher hasn't submitted grades yet | Wait for the grading period; ask teacher if overdue |
| Balance higher than expected | Previous year balance carried forward | View ledger for carry-forward entries; confirm with Accounting |
| Payment not reflected | Bank transfer pending validation | Wait 1–3 hours; check payment status in Ledger |
| Quiz not loading | Browser issue; poor internet | Refresh page; try a different browser; check internet connection |
| Materials not showing | Teacher may not have published them yet | Ask your teacher to confirm the material is published |
| Enrollment certificate shows wrong info | Registrar hasn't updated your record | Visit Registrar's office with supporting documents |

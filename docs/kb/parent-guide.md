## Role Overview

The **Parent Portal** keeps guardians informed and in control of their children's academic progress, school fees, and enrollment status — without having to visit the school for routine inquiries.

**What parents can do:**
- View each child's grades, report card, and class schedule
- Monitor school account balance and full payment history
- Pay school fees online (GCash, Maya, credit card, bank transfer)
- Initiate re-enrollment for the next school year
- Read class announcements and access learning materials
- Monitor flashcard quiz scores and study progress
- Manage multiple children from a single account

**What parents cannot do:**
- Change a child's personal information (must request at Registrar's office)
- Change a child's section or grade level
- View other families' data
- Modify grades or teacher submissions

## Workflow 1 — Logging In and Initial Setup

**URL:** `/portal-login`

---

### Step 1.1 — Receive Your Parent Account Credentials

A Parent account is created by the school when one of your children is enrolled. You will receive:
- **Username** — typically based on your name (e.g., `jdelacruz.parent`) or a system-assigned code
- **Initial Password** — a temporary password issued by the Registrar

Credentials are typically provided:
- At the Registrar's window when enrollment is completed, OR
- Via the contact number / email you provided during enrollment registration

If you have not received credentials and your child is already enrolled, approach the Registrar's office with a valid ID.

---

### Step 1.2 — First Login

1. Go to `/portal-login`
2. Enter your **username** and **initial password**
3. Click **Login** and select **Parent Portal** when prompted

---

### Step 1.3 — Update Your Contact Information

**Navigate to:** My Profile → Edit

Ensure your mobile number and email address are current. These are used for:
- Payment confirmation notifications
- School announcements via in-app notification
- Password reset

---

### Step 1.4 — Change Your Password

**Navigate to:** My Profile → Change Password

Choose a strong, private password. Do not use easily guessed passwords (birthday, child's name). If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, or approach the Registrar.

## Workflow 2 — Navigating the Parent Dashboard

**Navigate to:** `/parent` (after login)

The dashboard shows an overview of all your enrolled children:

| Widget | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| Children Summary | Cards for each enrolled child with name, grade, section, and balance |
| Recent Payments | Last few payments made on any child's account |
| Unread Announcements | Class announcements from each child's teachers |
| Upcoming Events | School calendar — examinations, holidays, activities |
| Pending Quizzes | Flashcard quizzes assigned to your children with approaching deadlines |

Click on a **child's card** to go to that child's detailed view.

## Workflow 3 — Viewing Your Child's Academic Records

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Grades

---

### Step 3.1 — View Grades by Period

Grades are organized by subject and grading period. Each subject shows:
- Quarter 1, Quarter 2, Quarter 3, Quarter 4 grades
- Final Grade (computed average)
- Pass/Fail status

Grades appear only after the subject teacher has officially **released** them. If a grading period appears blank, the teacher has not yet submitted those grades.

---

### Step 3.2 — View the Report Card

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Report Card

The report card shows all subjects with their final grades for the current or selected school year, in the official school format.

Click **Download PDF** to save a copy for your records.

---

### Step 3.3 — View Attendance Records

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Attendance

View your child's attendance summary across all enrolled subjects — days present, absent, late, and excused. If you dispute an attendance record, contact the subject teacher directly.

---

### Step 3.4 — View Academic History

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Academic History

See your child's complete grade history across all school years they have been enrolled. This is useful when preparing college applications, scholarship documents, or transfer requests.

## Workflow 4 — Monitoring Your Child's School Account Balance

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Ledger

---

### Step 4.1 — Understand the Ledger

The ledger is a complete timeline of your child's school account:

| Entry Type | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Charge** | A fee assessed by the school (tuition, miscellaneous, laboratory, etc.) |
| **Payment** | A payment made at the cashier's window or via online payment |
| **Discount** | A discount applied (scholarship, sibling discount, promo code) |
| **Carry-forward** | Balance from the previous school year that was unpaid |

The **Current Balance** at the top (or bottom) is the net amount your child currently owes.

---

### Step 4.2 — Download a Statement of Account

Click **Export / Print Statement** to download a formal statement of account. Useful for:
- Scholarship application supporting documents
- Verifying balance with the Accounting office
- Filing with your employer for educational benefits claims

---

## Workflow 5 — Making an Online Payment for Your Child

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Ledger → Pay Online

---

### Step 5.1 — Select Items to Pay

A list of your child's outstanding charges is shown. Select the items you wish to pay:
- You can pay all outstanding charges at once (full payment)
- Or select specific items (e.g., pay only tuition this month)

---

### Step 5.2 — Choose a Payment Channel

| Channel | How It Works |
|---------|-------------|
| **GCash** | Redirected to GCash payment page; uses your linked GCash account |
| **Maya** | Redirected to Maya payment page |
| **Credit / Debit Card** | Enter Visa or Mastercard details on the secure payment page |
| **Bank Transfer** | School's bank account details are shown; you transfer manually and upload proof |

All card and e-wallet transactions are processed through **PayMongo**, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. The school never stores your card details.

---

### Step 5.3A — E-Wallet or Card Payment Steps

1. Select the payment method and click **Proceed to Payment**.
2. Complete the payment on the secure payment page.
3. Return to the portal — your child's balance is updated **immediately**.
4. Receipt is available in Ledger → payment entry → View Receipt.

---

### Step 5.3B — Bank Transfer Steps

1. Note the displayed bank account details (name, account number, bank).
2. Open your bank's mobile app or online banking and transfer the exact amount.
3. Return to the portal, click **Upload Proof of Transfer**.
4. Upload a screenshot showing:
   - The reference/transaction number
   - The transferred amount
   - The recipient account name
   - The transaction date
5. Click **Submit for Validation**.

The school Cashier will review and approve the submission within **1–3 hours during school hours**. You will receive a portal notification once approved and your child's balance is updated.

---

### Step 5.4 — Keep Your Receipts

Official receipts are permanently stored in your child's Ledger. You can view and download any past receipt at any time. It is also good practice to screenshot payment confirmation pages immediately after each transaction.

## Workflow 6 — Applying a Discount Code

**When to do this:** If the school has provided you with a scholarship code, sibling discount code, or enrollment promotion code.

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Ledger → Apply Discount Code

---

### Step 6.1 — Enter the Code

Type the code exactly as given (case-sensitive). Click **Apply**. The system shows:
- Which specific fee items will be discounted
- The peso amount of the discount
- Your child's revised balance

---

### Step 6.2 — Confirm

Click **Confirm** to permanently apply. The discount is recorded in the ledger as a credit entry.

> **Important:** Codes are typically for one-time use. Once applied, they cannot be transferred to another student or reused. Contact Accounting if a code does not work as expected.

---

## Workflow 7 — Initiating Re-Enrollment for Your Child

**When to do this:** When the school opens re-enrollment for the next school year.

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Enrollment → Re-Enroll

---

### Step 7.1 — Verify Your Child's Information

The form shows your child's current enrollment details. Review that everything is accurate.

---

### Step 7.2 — Select Options (if applicable)

For Grade 10 students transitioning to Senior High School:
- Choose the preferred **Track** (Academic, Technical-Vocational, Sports, Arts & Design)
- Choose the preferred **Strand** (STEM, ABM, HUMSS, GAS, etc.)

---

### Step 7.3 — Submit the Re-Enrollment Request

Click **Submit**. Your child's enrollment status moves to `Pending Confirmation`. The Registrar reviews and confirms the enrollment, assigning a section.

After confirmation:
- Status updates to `For Assessment` → `For Payment` once fees are assigned
- You can then pay the enrollment fees through the portal (Workflow 5)

---

### Step 7.4 — Requirements Submission

After re-enrollment is submitted, check if any new requirements are needed for the next grade level:

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Enrollment → Requirements

Upload any required documents or note which ones must be brought physically to the Registrar.

## Workflow 8 — Viewing Learning Materials and Announcements

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Materials or Announcements

Your child's learning materials are organized by subject. You can view (but not download materials with restricted access) all content published by the teachers.

- Changes in class schedule
- Reminders about deadlines

## Workflow 9 — Monitoring Flashcard Quiz Performance

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard → [Child's Name] → Flashcards

---

### Step 9.1 — View Quiz Assignments

See all quiz decks assigned to your child by their teachers:
- Deck title and subject
- Deadline for completion
- Completion status (Not Started / In Progress / Completed)

---

### Step 9.2 — View Detailed Quiz Results

Click on a completed quiz to see:

- Number of attempts made
- Time taken

Use this information to identify subjects where your child may need additional study or tutoring support.

## Workflow 10 — Managing Multiple Children

If you have more than one child enrolled in the school, a single Parent account covers all of them.

**Navigate to:** Parent Dashboard

Each child appears as a separate card. Click the child's name or card to switch to their profile. All portal features (grades, ledger, payments, re-enrollment) are available for each child independently.

**Making a payment for multiple children in one session:**
- Each child's payment is done separately (navigate to Child A's ledger, pay; then navigate to Child B's ledger, pay).
- There is no combined multi-child payment in one transaction — this ensures each child's receipt is correct.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

| Problem | Possible Cause | What to Do |
|---------|---------------|------------|
| Child's name not showing in portal | Account not linked during enrollment | Visit Registrar with a valid ID to link the child to your account |
| Grades not showing | Teacher hasn't released grades yet | Grades are visible only after the teacher submits them — follow up with the teacher if overdue |
| Balance seems too high | Previous year carry-forward included | View ledger and look for "Carry-forward" entries; confirm with Accounting |
| Bank transfer not reflected after 4+ hours | Transfer may still be pending cashier review, or incorrect amount submitted | Check if you uploaded the correct proof of transfer; call the Accounting office during school hours |
| Discount code says "invalid" | Code expired, already used, or entered incorrectly | Double-check the code; contact Accounting if correct code not working |
| Cannot re-enroll online | Enrollment period not yet open, or your child has outstanding balance | Check if enrollment period is active; clear any balance first |
| Cannot log in | Wrong password | Use "Forgot Password" on the login page; or visit Registrar with ID |
